About Us

Developed in 2025 under CK97. Foundation, RamJanakiRail is Nepal's first train booking website, created by two students in a dorm room. This platform aims to modernize railway travel by providing a seamless and efficient online ticket booking system. RamJanakiRail primarily connects Janakpur, the capital city of State No. 2, with other major destinations across Nepal. With a focus on affordability, convenience, and punctuality, it offers modern coaches, competitive fares, and reliable scheduling, making train travel more accessible than ever.
